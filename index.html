How To Make Milk Tea

Tools :

  1. Glass with lid/shaker
  2. Glass

Ingredients :

  1. 1 sachet of milk powder
  2. 1 sachet of sweetened condensed milk
  3. 1 tablespoon of sugar
  4. 50 ml of hot water
  5. Ice cubes
  6. Full cream milk (optional)

Steps :

  1. First, prepare the hot water, then take 2 or 3 tea bags.
  2. Second, Brew the tea and wait for some minutes until the water blends.
  3. Prepare the glass with lid. Add milk poweder, sweetened condensed milk, and sugar.
  4. Don't forget to pour the tea and shake the ingridients.
  5. And then, add ice cubes into a glass. Pour the milk tea.
  6. This step is optional, you can do it if you want. Add full cream milk.
  7. The milk tea is ready to be served.
